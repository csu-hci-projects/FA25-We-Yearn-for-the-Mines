# We Yearn for the Mines

## A game developed by Benito Encarnacion and Parker Jones

We Yearn for the Mines is a 2D platformer game inspired by “Metroidvania” style games like Hollow Knight and Castlevania. 
The player spawns in a mysterious underground mine where they need to solve puzzles, fight enemies, and find hidden rooms 
to escape! The game’s strengths include its fluid player movement, unique enemies, bright visuals, and innovative shooting mechanic.  


### Instructions

We Yearn for the Mines has the following controls:  

* Move Left: A or Left Arrow Button 

* Move Right: D or Right Arrow Button 

* Jump: Space or Up Arrow  

* Crouch: Shift  

* Equip/unequip weapon: S 

* Shoot at enemies: Left Click 

* Shoot to destroy terrain: Right Click  

* Heal: Tab (-2 Moondust required to gain +1 Health)  

The player’s goal is to maneuver through the map, kill enemies, collect moondust (currency), and reach the end of the level 
(and discover any hidden areas along the way!). Current health and accumulated moondust are both visible at the top of the screen.
